# DCD-211 - Capacity utilization warning on the 12-week calendar

**Type:** Story
**Component:** Calendar
**Reported by:** Delivery Lead
**Priority:** Low
**Feature status:** Proposed (not built yet - nothing to observe in the app, analysis only)

## Description

When a resource is over-allocated, planners don't notice until someone does the math by hand or a delivery slips. Add a visual warning when a resource is at or over capacity in a given week on the 12-week calendar.

## Acceptance Criteria

- On the 12-week calendar (`/calendar/work_blocks_weekly`), a resource at or above 100% weekly utilization shows a warning indicator.
- Hovering the indicator shows used capacity vs total capacity for that week.

## Notes

Nice-to-have. Came up in a retro after we double-booked someone during a launch week.
